## Notes

### **Full Support**
- Native 120 Hz frame rate
- Active 3D support
- Frame-sequential output
- 4K per eye resolution

### **Partial Support**
- 60 Hz frame rate (requires frame doubling)
- Passive 3D support
- Lower resolution (requires upscaling)
- Requires MAGI Converter

### **No Support**
- No 3D support
- Low frame rate
- Incompatible format

## Conclusion

MAGI format is compatible with a wide range of devices, from professional cinema projectors to VR headsets. The best experience is achieved with devices that support native 120 Hz, active 3D, and 4K per eye resolution.

**For the best MAGI experience, we recommend:**
- **Professional Use:** Christie Mirage 4K, Barco DP4K-32B
- **Home Theater:** JVC DLA-NZ9, Epson Pro Cinema LS12000
- **Gaming:** NVIDIA RTX 4090 with 3D Vision
- **VR:** Apple Vision Pro

**The MAGI format is designed to be compatible with the widest range of devices possible!** 🚀
